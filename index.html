About the event

This is a unique leadership development programme that challenges leaders to make a real difference in the current real life problems, brainstorm & visualise next-generation out-turn. Participants learn about collaborative leadership with peers from other organisations through a live experience of collaboration.

During this facilitated programme 16 hours event, they create fresh ideas to solve real life challenges, with a touch of business slice in it. They learn practical tools and techniques, develop their own skills and behaviours and have the opportunity to network with leaders from different organisations. We have collaborated with “Co-Works Worli” and would be collaborating with NGO’s and other business enterprise

Ideation is here for you

Ideation 2019 is a social initiative undertaken by the IETE Student's forum and the Entrepreneurship Development Cell of SIES Graduate School of Technology in association with the Blue Ribbon and the Child Vision Foundation. This ideathon would primarily focus on solving the basic problems faced by the NGOs in their day-to-day activities. This premier initiative by the student chapters of SIES Graduate School of Technology is to inculcate the thoughts of social service in the minds of the youth.
